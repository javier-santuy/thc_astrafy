# MARKETING PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD
### **Strategic KPI Framework**

**Objective:** Track key performance indicators (KPIs) that will allow the marketing team to monitor business activity, identify trends in revenue fluctuations, and better understand their customer base of their e-commerce.

---

## 🟢 SPENDING EFFICIENCY
*Focus: Measuring the profitability and cost-effectiveness of advertising.*

### **1. Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**  
> **Definition:** The amount of revenue a business earns for every dollar spent on advertising. 
> **Formula:** `Total Revenue from Ads / Total Ad Spend` 

>### **2. Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** 
> **Definition:** How much it costs to gain one paying customer.  
> **Formula:**`Total Ad Spend / Number of New Customers` 

---

## 🔵 WEBSITE EFFECTIVENESS
*Focus: Optimizing the user journey.*

### **3. Conversion Rate (CR)**
> **Definition:** The percentage of sessions that result in a purchase.  
> **Formula:** `(Total Conversions / Total Sessions) * 100`

### **4. Cart Abandonment Rate**
> **Definition:** The percentage of shoppers who add items to a virtual cart but leave the site without purchasing.  
> **Formula:** `(1 - (Completed Transactions / Total Add to Carts)) * 100`

---

## 🟠 ATTRACTION
*Focus: Identifying where the high-value traffic originates.*

### **5. Traffic Source Breakdown**
> **Definition:** Shows where visitors are coming from (Direct, Organic Search, Paid Social, Email, Referral).  
> **Formula:** `% Source = (Sessions from Specific Source / Total Sessions) * 100`

---

## 🟣 CUSTOMER QUALITY
*Focus: Understanding long-term value and brand loyalty.*

### **6. New vs. Returning Customer Ratio**
> **Definition:** Compares the number of first-time buyers against customers who have made a purchase from your site in the past.  
> **Formula:** `Number of New Customers / Number of Returning Customers`

### **7. Average Order Value (AOV)**
> **Definition:** The average amount of money a customer spends every time they place an order.  
> **Formula:** `Total Revenue / Total Number of Orders`

### **8. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**
> **Definition:** The total revenue a business can reasonably expect from a single customer account throughout the business relationship.  
> **Formula:** `Average Order Value * Purchase Frequency * Customer Lifespan`

---

## 🚀 (Bonus): Sales Performance Forecasting
*Focus: Help the marketing team to have predictions for the upcoming week or month regarding sales performance.*

### **A. Sales Forecasting (Next 30 Days)**
> **Methodology:** Utilizing Time-Series models (like Prophet or Exponential Smoothing) to project future sales based on 2022-2023 historical data.
> 
> **Actionable Insight:** A visual "Confidence Interval" that warns the team if sales are projected to dip below the target, allowing them to trigger emergency promotions in advance.

### **B. Churn Risk & Reactivation**
> **Predictive Logic:** Identifying "Returning" or "VIP" customers who have not placed an order in the last 60 days (above their usual frequency).
> 
> **Actionable Insight:** Automated "Win-back" lists for targeted SMS or Email marketing to prevent high-value customers from leaving.
