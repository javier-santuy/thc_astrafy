## 🟢 SPENDING EFFICIENCY
*Focus: Measuring the profitability and cost-effectiveness of advertising.*

| KPI | Definition | Formula |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** | The amount of revenue a business earns for every dollar spent on advertising. | `Total Revenue from Ads / Total Ad Spend` |
| **2. Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** | How much it costs to gain one paying customer. | `Total Ad Spend / Number of New Customers` |

---

## 🔵 WEBSITE EFFECTIVENESS
*Focus: Optimizing the user journey and identifying friction points.*

### **3. Conversion Rate (CR)**
> **Definition:** The percentage of sessions that result in a purchase.  
> **Formula:** `(Total Conversions / Total Sessions) * 100`

### **4. Cart Abandonment Rate**
> **Definition:** The percentage of shoppers who add items to a virtual cart but leave the site without purchasing.  
> **Formula:** `(1 - (Completed Transactions / Total Add to Carts)) * 100`

---

## 🟠 ATTRACTION
*Focus: Identifying where your high-value traffic originates.*

### **5. Traffic Source Breakdown**
* **Definition:** Shows where visitors are coming from (Direct, Organic Search, Paid Social, Email, Referral).  
* **Formula:** `% Source = (Sessions from Specific Source / Total Sessions) * 100`

---

## 🟣 CUSTOMER QUALITY
*Focus: Understanding long-term value and brand loyalty.*

### **6. New vs. Returning Customer Ratio**
* **Definition:** Compares the number of first-time buyers against customers who have made a purchase from your site in the past.  
* **Formula:** `Number of New Customers / Number of Returning Customers`

### **7. Average Order Value (AOV)**
* **Definition:** The average amount of money a customer spends every time they place an order.  
* **Formula:** `Total Revenue / Total Number of Orders`

### **8. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**
* **Definition:** The total revenue a business can reasonably expect from a single customer account throughout the business relationship.  
* **Formula:** `Average Order Value * Purchase Frequency * Customer Lifespan`
